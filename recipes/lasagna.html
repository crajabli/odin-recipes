Lasagna

Directions

Fry the meat in the dutch oven. After doing so add your soffritto to the pan, and fry the vegetables until they are golden brown. Add the tomato paste, let it caramelize. Add red wine to deglaze the bottom of the pan. Pour can of peeled San Marzano tomates into the dutch oven. Put the fried meat back in the sauce and let it simmer for 2-3 hours.

To make the bechamel, melt the butter, and pour in the flour and mix while heat is on to make the roux. After the roux is done add in your milk and mix constantly. after a few minutes the mixture should be thick. Add your nutmeg and parmegianno reggiano to finish it of.

To assemble the lasagna, put a layer of bechamel on the bottom of the baking sheet. Then start layering your lasagna, first the pasta, then the ragu and then bechamel. Do that for a while until you reach your desired height. and at the end top it off with bechamel and parmegianno reggiano. Put it in the oven for 30 minutes at 180 degrees.

Ingredients

Ragu

  • 800g ground beef
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 rib of celery
  • 1 can of peeled San Marzano tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of thinly sliced garlic
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup of white wine
  • Pack of premade lasagna pasta

Bechamel

  • 1L of milk
  • 100g of butter
  • 100g of flour
  • 1/2 tsp of nutmeg
  • 100g of parmegianno reggiano
